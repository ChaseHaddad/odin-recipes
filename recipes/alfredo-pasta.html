Alfredo Pasta

Description

Fettuccine Alfredo is a pasta dish consisting of fettuccine tossed with butter and Parmesan cheese which melt and emulsify to form a rich cheese sauce coating the pasta.

Ingredients

  • 24 oz dry fettucine pasta
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 pint heavy cream
  • salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Romano cheese

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Melt butter into cream in a large saucepan over low heat; add salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Increase heat to medium; stir in grated Romano and Parmesan cheese until melted and sauce has thickened.
  4. Add cooked pasta to sauce and toss until thoroughly coated; serve immediately.
